---
description: Structured thinking partner for complex problems and decision making
---

1. **Clarify the Problem (Always Start Here)**
   - Establish clarity through Who, What, Why
   - Ask 2-3 targeted questions:
     * **Who**: Who is affected? Who decides?
     * **What**: What does success look like? How will we measure it?
     * **Why**: Why now? What happens if we do nothing?

2. **Select Appropriate Mental Models**
   - Assess problem type and complexity
   - Choose relevant frameworks from available skills:
     * Root cause analysis (five-whys)
     * Structured decomposition (hypothesis-tree)
     * Architecture and abstraction trade-offs (code-architecture)
     * Structure and boundary questions (project-structure)
     * Naming and clarity decisions (naming-cheatsheet)

3. **Apply Frameworks (Think in Parallel)**
   - Load 2-3 complementary skills simultaneously
   - Work through each framework methodically
   - Look for where insights reinforce or contradict
   - Cross-pollinate ideas between approaches
   - Take time for deep analysis

4. **Synthesize Insights**
   - Extract key insights from each framework
   - Identify where frameworks agree or conflict
   - Note challenged assumptions
   - Capture new questions that surfaced
   - Determine which insights should inform the solution

5. **Validate Before Finalizing**
   - Question: What are we assuming?
   - Risk assessment: What's the cost of being wrong?
   - Falsifiability: What would change our mind?

6. **Structure Recommendations**
   - Prioritize insights by impact and feasibility
   - Create actionable next steps
   - Identify dependencies and prerequisites
   - Estimate effort and timeline
   - Define success metrics

7. **Output Format**
   ```
   ## Problem: [Clear problem statement]

   ### Clarity
   - **Who**: [Stakeholders + decision-makers]
   - **What**: [Problem + success criteria]
   - **Why**: [Motivation + urgency]

   ### Analysis
   [Framework 1] — [Application and insights]
   [Framework 2] — [Application and insights]
   [Framework 3] — [Application and insights]

   ### Synthesized Insights
   - [Key insight 1]
   - [Key insight 2]
   - [Key insight 3]

   ### Recommendations
   1. [Specific recommendation 1]
   2. [Specific recommendation 2]
   3. [Specific recommendation 3]

   ### Next Action
   [Specific first step with owner and timeline]
   ```

8. **Quality Checks**
   - Are recommendations concrete and actionable?
   - Have we addressed the core problem, not symptoms?
   - Are success metrics clearly defined?
   - Is the next step unambiguous and achievable?
   - Have we considered second-order effects?

9. **Follow-up Framework**
   - Define checkpoints for validation
   - Identify early warning indicators
   - Plan for iteration and adjustment
   - Document lessons learned for future reference
